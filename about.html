About Thee Cigar Maestro

Thee Cigar Maestro was created by Certified Retail Tobacconist Mike Hamilton to elevate how we experience cigars — emotionally, ritually, and educationally.

Inspired by both tradition and modern technology, the platform blends verified cigar specs, emotional pairing logic, and GPT-powered education into one immersive system for cigar lovers, lounge owners, and learners.

Vision

To preserve the ritual of cigars while expanding access to knowledge, emotion, and community through intelligent tools that serve both old souls and new smokers alike.

Built For

  • Everyday Cigar Aficionados
  • Lounge Owners & Retail Staff
  • Educators, Event Hosts, and CEU Providers
  • Those who connect cigars with memory, legacy, and meaning
